Plenary is live: Watch live

The M​embers of the European Committee of the Regions will meet remotely for the 141st plenary session on 8, 9 and 10 December​ 2020.

Several debates and political discussions will take​ place, including the:

  • EU-UK Agreement and Territorial Consequences of Brexit, ​with the participation of ​​Michel Barnier, Head of Task Force for Relations with the United Kingdom
  • European Commission Work Programme 2021, with the participation of ​Maroš Šefčovič, Vice-president of the European Commission for Interinstitutional Relations and Foresight. 

Furthermore, there will be debates on: 

  • the Enlargement Package And European Neighbourhood Policy with Commissioner for Neighbourhood and Enlargement Olivér Várhelyi
  • a New Pact On Migration And Asylum with Commissioner for Home Affairs Ylva Johansson
  • the Revision Of The Common Agriculture Policy with Commissioner for Agriculture Janusz Wojciechowski
  • stepping up climate ambition in times of COVID-19 with the German Federal Minister for the Environment, Nature Conservation and Nuclear Safety Svenja Schulze.

​The plenary assembly will also discuss and vote​​ on the opinions listed below on this page.

There will also be a vote​​ on the following resolution: ​

  • Resolution on "The European Commission Work Programme 2021​" ​​​​
