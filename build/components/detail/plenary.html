    <section class="cor-l-plenary__main">
        <p>The M​embers of the European Committee of the Regions will meet remotely for the 141st plenary session on 8, 9 and 10 December​ 2020.</p>

        <p>Several debates and political discussions will take​ place, including the: </p>
        <ul>
            <li>EU-UK Agreement and Territorial Consequences of Brexit, ​with the participation of <strong>​​Michel Barnier</strong>, Head of Task Force for Relations with the United Kingdom </li>
            <li>European Commission Work Programme 2021, with the participation of <strong>​Maroš Šefčovič</strong>, Vice-president of the European Commission for Interinstitutional Relations and Foresight.  </li>
        </ul>
        <p>Furthermore, there will be debates on:  </p>
        <ul>
            <li>the Enlargement Package And European Neighbourhood Policy with Commissioner for Neighbourhood and Enlargement <strong>Olivér Várhelyi</strong> </li>
            <li>a New Pact On Migration And Asylum with Commissioner for Home Affairs <strong>Ylva Johansson</strong> </li>
            <li>the Revision Of The Common Agriculture Policy with Commissioner for Agriculture <strong>Janusz Wojciechowski</strong> </li>
            <li>stepping up climate ambition in times of COVID-19 with the German Federal Minister for the Environment, Nature Conservation and Nuclear Safety <strong>Svenja Schulze</strong>. </li>

        </ul>
        <p>​The plenary assembly will also discuss and vote​​ on the opinions listed <a href="">below on this page</a>. </p>
        <p>There will also be a vote​​ on the following resolution: ​ </p>
        <ul>
            <li>Resolution on "The European Commission Work Programme 2021​" ​​​​ </li>
        </ul>
    </section>
