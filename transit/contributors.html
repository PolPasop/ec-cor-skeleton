    Anthony Agotha

    Anthony (Tony) Agotha, is a Dutch career diplomat, who became Communication Advisor in the Cabinet of First Vice-President Timmermans in 2016, during the Juncker Commission. He was subsequently appointed Senior Diplomatic Expert in the new Cabinet of Executive Vice-President Timmermans, in the von der Leyen Commission. His current files include EU external policy, Brexit related issues, rule of law and deforestation.
